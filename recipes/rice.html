Rice Recipe

Try this simple rice recipe for dinner tonight! It's a very easy but so tasty side, and the leftovers are almost as good

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1/2 small onion, minced
  • 2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 pinch garlic salt, or to tast

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a nonstick saucepan over medium-high heat until almost smoking.
  2. Add rice; cook and stir quickly to toast, 2 to 3 minutes. Add onions and saut√© for 1 minute.
  3. Stir in broth and garlic salt; bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and cook until liquid is absorbed and rice is tender, about 20 minutes.
  5. Remove from the heat and allow to rest for 5 minutes. Uncover and fluff with a fork
