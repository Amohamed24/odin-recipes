French Toast Recipe

Enjoy our quick and easy French Toast recipe for a fuss-free breakfast during busy mornings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon McCormick速 All Natural Pure Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon McCormick速 Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 4 slices hearty sandwich bread

Steps

  1. Whisk egg, vanilla and cinnamon in shallow dish. Stir in milk.
  2. Dip bread slices in egg mixture, turning to coat evenly on both sides.
  3. Cook bread slices on lightly greased nonstick griddle or skillet on medium heat until cooked through and browned on both sides. Serve with Easy Spiced Syrup
  4. Add 1 teaspoon McCormick速 Pure Vanilla Extract and 1/4 teaspoon McCormick速 Ground Cinnamon to 1 cup pancake syrup; stir well to mix. Serve warm, if desired.
